- Sexual Health Links -

For actually useful advice about sexual health, talk to someone who won't make you feel embarrassed. There are a few sexual health clinics in Saigon. And there you will find helpful people who have heard it ALL. It's OK to feel shy, but don't hide it for too long. If something feels wrong with your body, there's no shame in talking about it. And if you have never told anyone about your same-sex relationship, these people will listen without judgement, and will keep your conversation confidential.

Pride & Prevention Center
Pride & Prevention Center (PPC World) is a sexual health clinic in District 1, Saigon. The people you meet here are open and kind people. They can test you for whichever STIs you are concerned you might have and also help you about to understand and respond appropriately to the results.
Galant Clinic
Galant Clinic has a few centers in different districts. As this is a clinic especially for sexual health, you will also be seen quickly here and can talk to helpful people about your sexuality and sexual concerns.
FV Hospital - Sexual Health Screening (vn)
FV Hospital - Sexual Health Screening (en)
FV Hospital offers screening for STDs in District 7. You may have to pay more to be seen here, as its a rather expensive private hospital. However, in some people's case, they may have additional services that are useful to you in addition to those found at smaller sexual health clinics.

Sexual Health Clinics (info)

Sexual Health Clinics are often run as small businesses. Many are very supportive of LGBT related issues around sex and sexuality (but don't panic, you can still go there if you are heterosexual as well). You can often get seen very quickly at these places, but it is still worth booking an appointment in advance, as opening hours can vary. Check out the websites for more information.

Sexual Health Screening @ Hospitals (info)

In Vietnam, not all hospitals offer Sexual Health services, but some do. Going to hospital for a screening may be easier for some people, but in general, clinics are good for focussed advice and support on sexual health in Vietnam.
