EVERYONE!


Mental health support is for EVERYONE. This webpage is just a reminder to people that if you are struggling, in any way, help is available. People who seek help aren't just one kind of crazy that you see in horror movies and they should be locked up... they are mostly normal people (although what is 'normal'?). This webpage provides links to services that are available, particularly to people living in Saigon and Vietnam.

There are all sorts of ways you can seek help, talk to a friend, talk to a trained professional - you could even try talking to an AI robot that will try to become your friend! Different methods work for different people and different issues.

"Should I see a therapist?"

Paying someone just to talk to them about yourself sounds weird when you think about it that way. But if you waste your money on things that might make you feel bad sometimes, then why not waste your money on something that might make you feel good. Do some research, find out what might work for you.

Counselling, CBT, psychotherapy, psychiatry are all very different things, and even within those categories there are many different approaches to how these people are trained and how they help people. Some use medication, some use none, some focus on your brain-chemistry, some focus on your unconscious mind, some methods seek to change your behaviour, some methods talk a lot about your parents; some help you to know yourself better, and some help you to unknow yourself; some methods use lots of framework at once, and some methods use no frameworks at all! Which one do you like the sound of? Do some research and find out.
